SUNRISE POOLS

Serving the Rogue Valley for 20 years. Sunrise Pool Service has been making pools shine, making our customers lives easier for a long time. You can trust Sunrise Pools to be there when you call.

Chemical Testing and Balancing

Your pool is an eco-system and a well balanced pool is a happy and safe pool. We know keeping the correct balance of chemicals is critical to both you and your pools.

Spa Tune Ups

We can also tune up your spa. We feature of range of spa services.

Waterworks

More than just a pool or spa? Sunrise Pools handles waterworks of all kinds. Call us today to see if we can help you.

Responsive Service

When you need us we'll be there. We know your pool is important and if you run into a problem you can be certain when you call us we'll answer!

Regular Maintenance

Regular Maintenance is the secret to shiny pools. Pools, like everything require regular and steady maintenance to remain shiny and clean. Our technicians will provide you with a regular balance of maintenance services so you can enjoy your pool!

Free Consultation

Step one? Call us now for a free consultation. We have years of experience so let us examine the pool, waterwork, or spa, and provide you with a recommendation today!
